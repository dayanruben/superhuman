Projects

IMO Bench

A suite of advanced benchmarks designed to evaluate robust mathematical reasoning in AI. Following our 2025 IMO gold medal achievement, this release includes:

  • IMO-AnswerBench: 400 challenging short-answer problems.
  • IMO-ProofBench: 60 proof-based problems vetted by experts.
  • IMO-GradingBench: A dataset of 1000 human gradings to advance automatic evaluation.
